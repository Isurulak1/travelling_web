Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 23, 2025

Welcome to TripSyncGo. We value your trust and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your information when you use our website, mobile app, or other services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our services:

  • Personal Information: Name, email, phone number, and payment details.
  • Usage Data: Interactions with our platform, clicks, and time spent.
  • Device Information: Browser type, IP address, and device identifiers.
  • Location Data: For location-based services such as local guides or transport.
  • Cookies & Tracking: To save preferences and analyze behavior.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To manage your bookings, accounts, and travel preferences.
  • To send updates, notifications, and promotional offers.
  • To enhance our platform performance and user experience.
  • To prevent unauthorized activities and maintain platform security.
  • To comply with regulatory and legal obligations.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. However, we may share it with:

  • Local transport providers, guides, and hotels to process your bookings.
  • Third-party vendors and service partners (under strict confidentiality agreements).
  • Government authorities when required for legal purposes.

4. Your Rights and Choices

  • You can review, update, or delete your personal information by contacting us.
  • You can unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time.
  • You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption and security protocols to protect your data. However, no system is completely secure—so we urge users to take precautions too (e.g., use strong passwords).

6. Children's Privacy

Our services are not designed for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If we discover such data, we will take immediate steps to remove it.

7. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may change from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes and always post the updated version here with the revision date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions, feedback, or requests related to your data, contact our support team at support@tripsyncgo.com.
