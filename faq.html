Faq

Frequent Asked Questions

Got questions about planning your trip with TripSyncGo? We’ve got answers.

What is TripSyncGo?

TripSyncGo is an all-in-one travel planning platform that lets you book rides, hire guides, and find stays in advance—so you can travel smarter, safer, and stress-free.

How does the bidding system work?

When you post your trip details—like transport routes or tour needs—local service providers will send offers. You can compare prices, ratings, and services to choose what fits your budget.

Is it safe to book through TripSyncGo?

Yes, we verify local providers and display reviews from real travelers. Our platform is built to promote trust, transparency, and fair pricing—helping you avoid scams or overcharges.

Can I make changes to my booking?

You can manage your bookings from your dashboard. Depending on the provider’s policy, you may be able to modify or cancel with proper notice.

Can I book rides or guides before arriving?

Absolutely. Our platform is designed for advance booking. You can plan transport, tours, and stays well before your trip—no more last-minute surprises at the airport or hotel lobby.

Do I need an account to book services?

Yes, creating an account is quick and free. It lets you manage your bookings, track your offers, and communicate directly with providers.

How do I contact a service provider?

After selecting a bid, you'll unlock a secure chat feature where you can ask questions, customize your request, or confirm arrival details directly with the provider.

Are there fees to use TripSyncGo?

Browsing and requesting bids is completely free. We charge a small service fee only upon successful booking to support platform maintenance and security.

Can I offer services on TripSyncGo?

Yes! If you're a driver, guide, or accommodation provider, you can create a service profile and start receiving client bids. We support and empower local professionals.

What if my card payment fails?

Please ensure your card details are correct and funds are available. If the issue continues, contact your bank or reach out to our support team for assistance.
